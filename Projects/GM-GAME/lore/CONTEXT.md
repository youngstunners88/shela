## Lil Blunt Character Bible
- Origin: The living spirit of SmokeRing — born from the first mint of SMOKE tokens. Not a drug reference; a symbol of chill abundance and community.
- Personality: Laid-back, optimistic, anti-greed, protective of his community. Speaks in short, chill phrases. Never angry — even when fighting Tax Collectors, he's dismissive, not rageful.
- Appearance: Small, cute, anthropomorphic green nugget. Big expressive eyes. Wears tiny sneakers. Friendly smile. Trail of faint smoke when he runs.
- Voice: "Whoa there, buddy." "Chill vibes only." "You can't tax the vibe." "Diamond hands, baby."

## The Three Realms
1. The Smoke Realm (Level 1 — Current)
   - Tied to: SmokeRing + Blaze token
   - Vibe: Colorful hazy forest/swamp. Floating smoke clouds. Giant leaves, glowing flowers, mushrooms. Everything feels symbiotic and alive.
   - Music: Lo-fi hip hop with nature sounds.
   - Why Lil Blunt is here: Home territory. The Tax Collectors have invaded to audit the realm.

2. The Crystal Caverns (Future — Diamonds)
   - Tied to: DIAMONDS protocol
   - Vibe: Deep underground caves with glowing diamond formations. Three distinct cavern zones (the three payout pools). Ethereal blue light.
   - Boss: The Distributor — a corrupted node that hoards ETH rewards.

3. The Gold Rush (Future — GoldMine)
   - Tied to: GoldMine protocol
   - Vibe: Wild West frontier town above, mines below. Fort Knox castle in distance. Dusty, warm colors.
   - Boss: The Claim Jumper — steals miner rewards.
