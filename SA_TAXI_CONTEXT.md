# South African Minibus Taxi Context - Bhubezi

## Industry Overview
- **200,000+ minibus taxis** operate nationally
- **15 million passengers daily**
- **$1.2+ billion annual revenue**
- **60% of road commuting** in SA is via taxi
- Industry dates back to **1980s** (deregulated in 1987)

## Key Terminology (Authentic SA)

### Vehicles
- **Taxi / Quantum** - The minibus (Toyota Quantum most common)
- **Combis** - Older term for minibus taxis
- **Sprinter** - Mercedes Sprinter vans
- **Impila** - Nissan Impendulo

### Locations
- **Rank** - Taxi rank (depot/station)
- **Jozi / eGoli** - Johannesburg
- **Bara** - Chris Hani Baragwanath Hospital (Soweto)
- **CBD** - City Center
- **Township** - Residential areas (Soweto, Alex, etc.)

### Operations
- **Association** - Taxi association controlling routes
- **Route** - Specific path (e.g., Bree to Bara)
- **Marshal** - Rank manager/controller
- **Guardjie** - Rank assistant
- **Bree** - Bree Street Taxi Rank (main Jozi rank)
- **Noord** - Noord Street Taxi Rank

### Slang/Terms
- **Sharp sharp** - Everything good/ok
- **Sho** - Hi/Hey
- **Baba** - Father/respected elder
- **Malume** - Uncle
- **Sis** - Sister
- **Bra** - Brother/friend
- **Yebo** - Yes
- **Lekker** - Good/nice
- **Now now** - Soon (could mean now or later)
- **Just now** - In a while

## Payment Reality
- **NO digital payments** - Cash only (mafia-controlled)
- Fares range **R15-R50** depending on distance
- Payment happens **inside the taxi** to driver
- App is for **information/tracking only**, NOT payment

## Route Structure
Routes are fiercely controlled by associations:
- Joining fee: **R30,000 - R120,000**
- Monthly fees to association
- Violence between competing associations common
- "Turf wars" over lucrative routes

## Key Ranks (Johannesburg)
1. **Bree Street** - CBD, major hub
2. **Noord Street** - CBD, Soweto routes
3. **Park Station** - CBD, long distance
4. **MTN Rank** - Noord extension
5. **Wanderers** - CBD, eastern routes

## Routes (Examples)
- Bree → Bara (Soweto)
- Noord → Dobsonville
- Bree → Tembisa
- Park → Pretoria
- Bree → Alex

## User Roles in Bhubezi
1. **Passenger** - Finds routes, tracks taxis, asks prices
2. **Driver** - Updates status, sees passenger pings
3. **Marshal** - Manages rank flow, approves drivers

## App Features (Contextualized)
- Route price checking (community-verified)
- Taxi location tracking
- Rank status updates (busy/quiet)
- Community chat (per route/rank)
- Driver verification (photo-based)
- Social feed (Tavern) for updates
- Points/gamification for contributions

## UI/UX Considerations
- High contrast (sunlight visibility)
- Large touch targets (gloves, rough hands)
- Low data usage (township connectivity)
- Works offline (sync when connected)
- Simple language (isiZulu, isiXhosa, English mix)
- Yellow/black branding (taxi colors)
