## Overview

(OFT) Omni-Chain Fungible Token on BASE, ETH, & BSC. Also on PLS via PulseChain's Native Bridge from Ethereum.

## Key Details

- **OFT SMOKE** trades on BASE, ETH, BSC, and PulseChain.
- **Main LP's** will be, SMOKE/X28 on both BASE & ETH and SMOKE/BNB on Binance.
- **Liquidity** in LP's will be 10 Billion SMOKE to 14 Billion X28 on BASE & ETH and locked (10yr) on each chain. 5 billion SMOKE and 1.2 BNB on Binance.
- **Original SMOKE** holders of the TAX/Reflections token can use the MINT to convert OG SMOKE to Non Taxed SMOKE for a 1:1 mint.
- **100% of the OG SMOKE** used to mint the OFT SMOKE will be sent directly to the H420 buy and burn contract on Helios app: https://helios.win/buyburn
