# Lil Blunt Lore Bible

## Enemy Symbolism

### Greedy Tax Collectors
Represent bureaucratic overhead, excessive taxation, establishment greed. Small creatures in suits carrying clipboards.

### Fly Swarms
Represent annoyance, FUD, petty distractions. Buzz around and waste your time.

### Rolling Boulders
Represent market crashes. Unstoppable force you must outmaneuver.

### Hostile Vines
Represent regulatory capture. Systems that should help but have been corrupted to harm.

---

## The Auditor (Boss)

A massive Tax Collector in a top hat and monocle.

Symbolizes the ultimate establishment force trying to shut down decentralized communities.

Defeating him = protecting the realm's autonomy.

## Tone Guidelines

- Lore documents read like a game bible, not a whitepaper
- Every protocol reference is woven into gameplay language, not pasted on
- Tone stays chill and positive — even enemies are pathetic/greedy, not evil
- Marketing copy can be extracted directly from lore docs
