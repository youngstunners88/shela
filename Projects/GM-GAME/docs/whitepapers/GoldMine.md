## Overview

Do you believe in Bitcoin as the digital gold of this era, or do you prefer the security of XAUT, real gold-backed tokens?

**GoldMine offers both** — a gamified DeFi mining, staking, and auction platform that combines on-chain mechanics with strategic real-world asset investments in Bitcoin mining and gold streaming.

## Protocol Highlights

GOLD MINE is a gamified DeFi mining, staking, and auction platform designed to create sustainable value through structured token mechanics and strategic real-world asset activities.

Users start fixed **100-day Miners** by paying with ETH (and optionally Diamonds). They earn GOLD tokens at a rate of **1% per day** and can claim early, forfeiting any unearned portion.

### Fund Allocation (100% of mining proceeds)

| Allocation | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **35%** | Fort Knox staking pools (88-day and 288-day) — stake GOLD to earn wBTC rewards |
| **25%** | Weekly GOLD Rush Auctions — deposit mined GOLD to compete for XAUT |
| **15%** | Treasury / Sovereign Wealth Fund |
| **10%** | Permanent burn of Diamonds (deflationary mechanism) |
| **5%** | GOLD Stockpile liquidity (GOLD/wBTC pool) |
| **3%** | Marketing Budget |
| **3%** | Founder Allocation |
| **3%** | Build Cost |
| **1%** | Protocol Maintenance |

## Future — Phase 2 (Coming Soon)

### Sovereign Wealth Fund
The Treasury will house the Sovereign Wealth Fund, which will manage liquidity provision from Diamonds sales and serve as the central portal for interactions with a separate legal entity responsible for real-world asset investments.

### Gold Certificate NFTs
Non-transferable membership passes available to users who meet specific long-term Fort Knox staking requirements. These NFTs provide eligibility for consideration in certain XAUT distribution pools associated with the protocol's gold-related activities.

### Real-World Asset Investments
The Sovereign Wealth Fund will support real Proof-of-Work Bitcoin mining investments and gold streaming/royalty deals. Proceeds will provide supplemental XAUT to weekly GOLD Rush Auctions and supplemental wBTC to Fort Knox staking pools.

## Key Details

- The only liquidity pool in the protocol is the **wBTC pair on Ethereum**.
- System is designed to be self-sustaining with Wild West gold rush theme.
- Multiple participation paths: short-term auctions and long-term staking.
