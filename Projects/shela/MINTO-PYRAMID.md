# Shela Hackathon Submission — Minto Pyramid Structure

## ONE-SENTENCE CORE THESIS

**"Shela is a dating safety protocol that uses AI interviews, crypto staking, and privacy-preserving verification to make meeting strangers safer — and learns from every outcome."**

---

## SUPPORTING ARGUMENTS (2-4 MECE)

### Argument 1: SAFETY-FIRST DESIGN
**Core:** Current dating apps match first, vet later (if at all)

**Evidence:**
• 5-layer protocol: AI interview → risk-weighted matching → crypto escrow → geofenced verification → pattern learning
• Women bear 90%+ of safety burden in current apps — Shela shifts burden to system
• Risk scores 0-100 determine interaction tiers (text/voice/video/meet)

---

### Argument 2: CRYPTO ECONOMICS THAT ALIGN INCENTIVES
**Core:** Staking creates real financial consequences for bad behavior

**Evidence:**
• Stake tiers: Text ($0.80) → Voice ($1.28) → Video ($1.60) → Meet ($16)
• No-show = stake slashed (30-100% based on violation history)
• Reputation NFTs: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond (stake discounts up to 50%)

---

### Argument 3: PRIVACY-BY-DESIGN
**Core:** Verify without exposing

**Evidence:**
• ZK location proofs — prove you're at meet spot without revealing exact coordinates
• Blur-faced photos stored temporarily, deleted after verification
• 500m geofence radius (generous for privacy, strict enough for safety)

---

### Argument 4: CONTINUOUS LEARNING
**Core:** System gets smarter with every meet

**Evidence:**
• Pattern detection: ghosters, safety concerns, reliable users
• Interview prompts evolve based on violation patterns
• Risk model updates every 6 hours from real outcomes
