# 🚕 Bhubezi - Jozi Taxi Network

> South Africa's #1 Minibus Taxi App - Track routes, find rides, and earn rewards.

![Bhubezi Logo](frontend/public/bhubezi-icon.svg)

---

## What is Bhubezi?

Bhubezi is a community-powered app for Johannesburg's minibus taxi industry. It helps passengers find routes, drivers fill seats, and marshals manage ranks - all while earning rewards for contributing.

### Why "Bhubezi"?
"Bhubezi" means "lion" in isiZulu - representing strength, pride, and leadership in the Jozi taxi community.

---

## 🎯 Features

### For Passengers
- 📍 Find taxis on your route
- 💰 Check community-verified prices
- 📊 See rank status (busy/quiet)
- 💬 Chat with drivers
- 🔔 Get alerts for your route

### For Drivers
- 📍 Share your location
- 👥 See passenger pings
- 💬 Communicate with ranks
- 🏆 Earn points for contributions
- 📸 Upload taxi wash photos

### For Marshals
- 📊 Update rank status
- ✅ Approve drivers
- 🚨 Send route alerts
- 📈 Monitor activity

## 🌍 South African Context

This app is specifically designed for SA's unique minibus taxi industry:
- **No payment system** - Cash only (industry standard)
- **Association-based** - Routes controlled by taxi associations
- **Rank culture** - Taxis operate from designated ranks
- **Community verified** - Prices/info verified by users

### Key Terms
- **Rank** - Taxi station (Bree, Noord, Park Station)
- **Quantum** - Toyota Quantum minibus
- **Sharp sharp** - Everything good
- **Combis** - Minibus taxis
- **Marshal** - Rank manager
